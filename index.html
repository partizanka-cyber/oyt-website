The Full Process

First Call to First Day.

You Book a Complimentary Strategy Call

A complimentary strategy session focused on role mapping. We map your team structure, uncover the bottlenecks, and define the exact role that will move the needle most. You walk away with clarity before we ever start searching.

We Manage the Full Search

We write the job description, pay for the ads, and search the full global market from scratch. Most agencies pull from a database they already have. We go out fresh every time. We prequalify and personally interview the top candidates. You do none of this.

You Review the Shortlist

We send you a shortlist of candidates with their resumes, DISC profiles, and recorded video interviews. You review on your own time and choose your favorites.

We Schedule the Interviews

We coordinate the interviews between you and your top picks so you can meet them, ask your questions, and make a confident final decision.

You Hire Them Directly

Onboard them directly or through a third-party payroll provider. Either way, they work exclusively for you and become a dedicated part of your team.

We Hand You the Onboarding Guide

We provide a full onboarding and management guide so you know exactly how to set them up, integrate them into your team, and keep them long term.
